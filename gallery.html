Sports

Participation in sport, at least 3 times a week, is compulsory for every learner. Kitsong teams compete locally in soccer, netball, basketball and athletics. Kitsong learners have excelled in sports, with many learners representing regional, provincial and national teams.

In the prestigious Junior NBA basketball tournament, Kitsong won two Champions Trophies in 2017 and 2018. One of our learners was subsequently offered a scholarship to study in America as a result of the excellent participation in the sport.

Culture

Kitsong has a vibrant cultural curriculum involving drama, film, debating and singing.

Kitsong learners have become widely known for their short film productions, which are scripted, acted, filmed

The film "I have something to tell you " Won Best Film in the 12 - 18 year old category at an international film festival in Philadelphia, USA.

In the first half of 2019, 6 short films were produced and are being entered into a range of festivals nationally and internationally.

In 2021 our short film; Find me by the River was nominated in Africa Movie Academy Awards (AMAA) held in Nigeria Lagos. The Film higlighted the impact and issues around Bullying in schools and around us.
