ABOUT KITSONG SCHOOLS OF THE ROYAL BAFOKENG NATION

Affordable high quality education for all children.

Kitsong School is an independent school that was established in 2015 to make quality education accessible to all children in the Royal Bafokeng Nation(RBN). This School is under Royal Bafokeng Institute (RBI).

EXPLORE NOW

Education

Kitsong employs highly qualified graduate teachers who are passionate about education and demonstrate innovation and enthusiasm in everything they do.

The school facilities are well-equipped to support our stimulating curriculum, with a particular focus on mathematics and science.

The school currently offers Grades R–4 and 8–12 and has been in the process of expanding since 2020. It plans to offer Grade 5 in 2024, Grade 6 and Grade 7 by 2026.

Curriculum

One of the most critical skills shortages in South Africa is in Science and Technology, particularly Engineering.

Kitsong school provides a CURRICULUM ASSESSMENT POLICY STATEMENT (CAPS) programme that is strongly focused on mathematics, Science, Information Technology and Languages, but it also gives its scholars excellent preparation for any tertiary study in South Africa or worldwide.

Our learners write the Independent Examinations Board (IEB) examinations.

Information Technology

Every learner is required to have a tablet which they use in class under the guidance of teachers for various tasks. Classrooms are equipped with data projectors and whiteboards and learners are taught in a blended environment.

Our assessments are written via computers and also through other traditional methods.
